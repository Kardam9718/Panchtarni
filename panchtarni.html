Our Story

A Dream Born from
the Heart of India

"Har roti mein ek sapna — ki har ghar mein shudh, poshtik, aur swadisht atta pahunche."

Panchtarni was founded in January 2026 with a clear and heartfelt mission: to bring genuinely pure, nutritious, and chakki-fresh whole wheat atta to every Indian household. Our journey started with a commitment — no compromise on quality, no shortcuts, and no mixing.

We source only the finest golden wheat from the fields of Madhya Pradesh, grind it using state-of-the-art chakki technology in a hygienic, fully enclosed facility, and deliver it fresh to your doorstep — retaining every grain of nutrition.

Satyapal Singh Co-Founder, Panchtarni Atta

Why Choose Us

The Panchtarni Promise

Every step of our process is designed with one goal — to deliver the purest, most nourishing atta to your family's table.

Modern Chakki Process

Our state-of-the-art stone grinding preserves the natural bran and germ — giving you atta that is not just flour, but wholesome nutrition.

Hygienic Manufacturing

Produced in a clean, enclosed, and pest-free environment. Every batch is quality-checked before packaging so your health is never compromised.
